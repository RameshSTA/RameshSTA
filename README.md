### 🔷 Apex — Revenue Intelligence Platform

<div align="center">

[![Live Dashboard](https://img.shields.io/badge/📊_Live_Dashboard-D4A853?style=for-the-badge)](https://RameshSTA.github.io/apex)
[![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub_Repo-181717?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/RameshSTA/apex)

</div>

> Airline revenue-management intelligence solving demand forecasting, LP cabin-mix optimisation, causal price-elasticity identification, and agentic RM automation — grounded in Qantas Group's A$23.8B FY2025 operational scale.

<div align="center">

| 📈 7.3% Revenue Uplift | 🎯 < 14% MAPE | ⚡ < 1 ms LP Solve | 🤖 < 300 ms Agent |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| HiGHS LP Optimiser | 10 AU Domestic Routes | scipy HiGHS backend | Brief → Recommendation |

</div>

**What it does:**
- 📡 **Hybrid Forecasting** — Holt-Winters + GBT residual model on 30 engineered features; walk-forward CV + bootstrap CI across 10 Australian domestic routes
- ⚙️ **LP Optimisation** — scipy HiGHS linear programme maximises cabin-mix revenue; dual variable recovery produces defensible bid prices replacing static quarterly targets
- 🔬 **Causal Inference** — DiD (parallel-trends placebo) quantifies competitor-entry yield impact; 2SLS IV (IATA jet-fuel index) corrects OLS simultaneity bias in elasticity estimation
- 🧠 **Agentic Orchestration** — Tool-calling agent plans forecast → optimise chains from plain-language brief with fully auditable recommendation trail in < 300 ms

`Python 3.10+` `Holt-Winters` `GradientBoosting` `scikit-learn` `scipy HiGHS` `NumPy` `Difference-in-Differences` `2SLS IV` `Chart.js`
